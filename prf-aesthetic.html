About the Treatment

What is PRF Skin Treatment?

Platelet-Rich Fibrin (PRF) is a second-generation platelet concentrate derived from your own blood. Unlike traditional PRP, PRF forms a natural fibrin matrix that acts as a biological scaffold, enabling a sustained and gradual release of growth factors over 10 to 14 days.

This extended release profile promotes deeper tissue regeneration, stimulating collagen and elastin production at a cellular level. The result is progressive, natural-looking skin rejuvenation that improves over weeks and months.

Because PRF is 100% autologous — made entirely from your own blood — there is virtually no risk of allergic reaction or rejection. It represents the pinnacle of natural, evidence-based aesthetic medicine.

PRF vs PRP: The Key Difference

While PRP releases growth factors within hours, PRF's fibrin matrix sustains release over 10–14 days, providing significantly deeper and longer-lasting tissue regeneration. This makes PRF the superior choice for lasting aesthetic results.

Your Treatment Journey

The PRF Process

Consultation

A thorough skin assessment and discussion of your aesthetic goals. We create a personalised treatment plan tailored to your skin type, concerns, and desired outcomes.

Blood Draw

A small sample of your blood is collected, similar to a routine blood test. This is a quick and straightforward process that takes just a few minutes.

PRF Preparation

Your blood is processed using a specialised low-speed centrifugation protocol to isolate the platelet-rich fibrin matrix, concentrating your body's natural growth factors.

Treatment

The PRF is precisely injected or applied to the target areas using advanced techniques. A topical anaesthetic ensures your comfort throughout the procedure.

What to Expect

Results & Expectations

Results Timeline

Initial glow is visible within 1–2 weeks. Collagen remodelling continues over 3–6 months, with peak results typically seen at the 3-month mark. Skin continues to improve progressively.

Recommended Sessions

For optimal results, we recommend an initial course of 3–4 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart. This allows each treatment to build upon the regenerative effects of the previous one.

Maintenance

After your initial course, maintenance sessions every 6–12 months help sustain results. Your practitioner will create a long-term plan tailored to your skin's ongoing needs.

PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin) is the next generation of PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma). The result is deeper, more sustained tissue regeneration and superior clinical outcomes.

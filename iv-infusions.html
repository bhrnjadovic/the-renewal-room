The Science

Why IV Infusion Therapy?

Intravenous nutrient therapy delivers vitamins, minerals, and amino acids directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the gastrointestinal tract entirely. This means your body receives the full therapeutic dose without the limitations of oral absorption.

Every IV protocol at SkinSci is medically supervised and customised to your individual health profile. Whether you are seeking enhanced energy, immune support, athletic recovery, or skin rejuvenation, our formulations are designed for measurable clinical outcomes.

We use only pharmaceutical-grade ingredients sourced from TGA-approved suppliers, ensuring the highest safety and efficacy standards for every infusion.

100% Bioavailability

IV delivery achieves 100% bioavailability compared to just 20–50% with oral supplements. This means your cells receive the nutrients they need at therapeutic concentrations.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, IV infusion therapy is very safe when administered by qualified medical professionals. At SkinSci, all infusions are delivered by AHPRA-registered practitioners using TGA-approved pharmaceutical-grade ingredients. We conduct a thorough health assessment before your first session and monitor your vital signs throughout every treatment. Our clinic is fully equipped with emergency protocols and resuscitation equipment.

Most IV infusion sessions take between 30 and 60 minutes, depending on the specific protocol and drip rate. NAD+ infusions may take longer, typically 60 to 90 minutes, as they are administered at a slower rate for optimal comfort and absorption. We recommend allowing approximately 90 minutes for your overall appointment including the initial assessment.

Frequency depends on your individual goals and health status. For general wellness and maintenance, many patients benefit from monthly sessions. Those addressing specific deficiencies or undergoing intensive protocols may start with weekly sessions before transitioning to a maintenance schedule. Your practitioner will recommend a personalised treatment plan during your consultation.

Many patients report feeling more energised and alert within hours of their infusion. Some effects, particularly for skin health and immune support, build progressively over a series of sessions. Energy-focused infusions like PERFORM tend to have the most immediately noticeable effects, while protocols like NAD+ deliver cumulative benefits that become more apparent over several weeks of treatment.

Side effects are rare and generally mild. Some patients may experience slight coolness at the infusion site, a mild flush during high-dose vitamin C delivery, or temporary lightheadedness. NAD+ infusions can occasionally cause mild nausea or chest tightness, which is managed by adjusting the drip rate. All potential effects are discussed during your pre-treatment assessment, and our team monitors you throughout your session.

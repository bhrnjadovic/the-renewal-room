Our Location

Find Us in Balmain

Map — Balmain, Sydney NSW 2041

Our clinic is located in the heart of Balmain, one of Sydney's most charming inner-west villages.

Easily accessible via the 441, 442, and 433 bus routes from the CBD. Street parking is available on surrounding streets, and metered parking can be found along Darling Street. We're just a short walk from the Balmain East ferry wharf.

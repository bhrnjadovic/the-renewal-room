Our Approach

Our Aesthetic Philosophy

At SkinSci, we believe the finest aesthetic results are those that enhance your natural beauty rather than alter it. Our approach combines evidence-based science with a deep understanding of facial anatomy and skin physiology to deliver subtle, refined outcomes.

Our Treatments

Advanced Aesthetic Treatments

Clinically proven, non-surgical solutions tailored to your skin's unique needs and your aesthetic goals.

Microneedling

Controlled micro-injuries stimulate your skin's natural collagen induction cascade, triggering deep repair and remodelling from within.

Best for: Fine lines, acne scarring, uneven skin texture

RF Microneedling

Combines radiofrequency energy with precision microneedling to deliver thermal energy deep into the dermis for superior skin tightening and collagen remodelling.

Best for: Skin tightening, deep wrinkles, laxity

IPL Therapy

Intense pulsed light therapy targets chromophores in the skin, effectively treating pigmentation irregularities, redness, and vascular concerns.

Best for: Pigmentation, redness, vascular lesions

Laser Treatments

Fractional and picosecond laser technologies for precise skin resurfacing, pigmentation correction, and stimulation of new collagen formation.

Best for: Pigmentation, resurfacing, skin renewal

LED Light Therapy

Specific wavelengths of light energy penetrate the skin to reduce inflammation, accelerate wound healing, and promote cellular repair at a mitochondrial level.

Best for: Inflammation, post-treatment recovery, cellular repair

Skin Boosters & Bio-Remodelling

Injectable hyaluronic acid micro-deposits that deeply hydrate the skin from within, improving elasticity, luminosity, and overall skin quality over time.

Best for: Deep hydration, skin quality, radiance

PDO Thread Lifting

Dissolvable polydioxanone threads are precisely placed beneath the skin to provide an immediate lifting effect while stimulating natural collagen production for a non-surgical facelift.

Best for: Non-surgical lifting, jawline definition, sagging

Chemical Peels

Medical-grade chemical peels remove damaged outer layers of skin to reveal fresher, smoother skin beneath. Customised acid blends target specific concerns at controlled depths.

Best for: Skin renewal, congestion, dullness, uneven tone
